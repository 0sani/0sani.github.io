61B Debug Checklist

Before Asking Anything

  1. Read the instructions again; is your question answered there?
  2. Have you looked at the getting started slides/videos (if applicable)?
  3. Have you used the debugger?
  4. Have you looked at the Java visualizer?

Posing the Question

  1. What is the issue? Be specific
  2. Where do you think the issue is?
  3. What have you tried to do to debug it?
